Legal Information

1. Purpose
In this study we investigate how we perceive utterances in conversations. By participating, you will get an insight into our research. If you are interested, we will be happy to explain the exact goals of this study to you afterwards. You will further help us to gain new insights into human cognition.
2. Procedure
In the following, you will receive detailed information regarding the procedure of the study. (details on procedure, duration and collected data)
3. Confidentiality
We save the data under a randomly assigned participant number and thus totally anonymized on a server of Tübingen University. All data collected during this study will be treated confidentially. All people that have access to your personal data are obliged to maintain data secrecy. If data from this study is published, this will happen in an anonymized manner. Hence, your data cannot be assigned to you. If the results from this study are going to be published in a scientific publication, we will save the data in an online database (hosted by the open science foundation, OSF or Clarin-D). This database was established for the safe storage of anonymized research data. If the study does not result in a scientific publication, we will store the data on a password-protected university server for a maximum of 10 years. The data is treated confidentially and is used only for scientific purposes. Since the data is stored completely anonymized, we cannot delete the collected data of individuals afterwards. You are free to withdraw at any time, in this case, recorded data will not be used, we will delete it before the analysis begins (cf. point 5).
4. Compensation
You will receive 2£ for your participation.
5. Voluntary participation
Your participation in this research is entirely voluntary. There will be no negative repercussions if you withdraw from the study.
6. Right to refuse or withdraw
You do not have to take part in this study if you do not wish to do so. You are free to withdraw at any time. You do not need to provide a reason for your withdrawal. If you withdraw from the study before data collection is completed, your data will not be used. You can return the study on Prolific, and your score will not be negatively affected. You are allowed to return the study both before you begin the experiment (click “Cancel Reservation”) or during the experiment (click “Stop without Completing"). Please note that a compensation will not be possible if you cancel your participation before the end of the study.
7. Risks
Your participation does not involve any physical or emotional risk to you. You are free to withdraw at any time.
8. Insurance coverage
Not necessary for participating in this study.
9. Responsible contact
If you have questions or if there are any problems, please contact us via asya.achimova@uni-tuebingen.de. 10. Data sharing
We would like to share this data with other researchers. In order to share data, we publish it on our website of Open Science Foundation or Clarin-D under a creative commons license (CC-BY), to be found under: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/2.0/legalcode. These services provide secure storage of anonymized research data. You can choose how much you allow us to share. The more you allow us to share, the bigger the contribution to science!
